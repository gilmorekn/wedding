How to Fall in Love

Go contra dancing at Blowing Rock Legion Hall . Have a first date at Local Lion. Climb at Center 45. Play cribbage at AMB . Go on a hike at Green Knob and grab a beer at Blowing Rock Ale House afterwards. Climb at Blowing Rock Boulders with a friend. Dog sit Fern. Go to church at Boone UMC . Have lots of reading dates at the tree on the hill at Moses Cone (propose here later). Design and execute a progressive dinner on King Street ( The Horton, COBO, Venture, The Local) followed by a silent disco. Climb some more at Raven Rock Boulders with friends. Park a truck and watch the sunset at Grand View Overlook. Go ice skating at App Ski Mountain and walk around the festival of the lights at Chetola.
